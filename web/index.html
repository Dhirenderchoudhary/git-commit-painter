How It Works

Three steps to pixel art on your profile.

01

Draw or Type

Use the pixel canvas to draw freehand art, or type text to auto-generate a pattern.

02

Generate Command

Enter your GitHub username and repo, then copy the generated CLI command.

git-commit-painter -t "hello" -m 10 --push --origin <url>
03

See Your Art

Run the command, push to GitHub, and your contribution graph turns into pixel art.
